Notice to Prospective Students:

I will be recruiting PhD students to start in Fall 2020. If you are a prospective PhD student interested in working with me, please apply to the UMSI PhD program.

If you are a current UM masters student or undergraduate student interested in working with me, please email me with your CV/resume and research interests.

I am particularly interested in working with students who want to conduct research on one or more of the following topics:

  1. Life Transitions and Social Technologies
  2. Transgender and/or LGBQ+ people and Social Technologies
  3. Social Media Content Moderation and Marginalized Populations.
