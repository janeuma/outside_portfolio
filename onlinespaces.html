Online Spaces and Sensitive Populations


Online spaces can be important platforms for people from sensitive, stigmatized, and vulnerable populations to discuss their problems and find support; yet platform design decisions impact how people present themselves and interact with others.

By studying these groups' online self-presentation and disclosure practices, along with the politics of social media platform design, we can understand how to design technology to better support those facing difficult issues like sexual abuse, relationship breakups, eating disorders, depression, and gender presentation.
